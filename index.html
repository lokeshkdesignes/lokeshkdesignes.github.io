Graphic Designer & DI Colorist

I'm a Graphic Designer and DI Colorist who truly enjoys bringing stories to life through visuals. Whether it's designing a poster or adjusting colors in a film, I love turning ideas into something people can see and feel.

I'm passionate about creativity, and I always aim to make my work meaningful, not just good-looking. With experience in both design and post-production, I've learned how to blend technical skills with a creative eye to make visuals that connect with people.
